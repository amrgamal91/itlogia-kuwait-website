About us

itlogia was established in 2009 as a IT Company focusing on providing middle to large organization with IT Solutions that fill the gap of monitoring there IT Infrastructure, our slogan is Service is Money; Keep IT up to save it. itlogia is a solutions and services provider for end to end service monitoring for IT Operations, Applications and Storage.

itlogia Kuwait provides industry leading solution in network and system and automation space with capability to automatically analyses problems across virtual, logical or physical objects in large complex environments and provides automated cross-domain monitoring of availability and performance with automated root-cause and impact analysis across network devices, servers, applications, storage and security domains across physical and virtual environment.
